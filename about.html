cHi mE No is a Lisbon based Communication Designer who seeks to bridge the gap between experimentation, structure and contemporaneity with her design practice. Enticed by the ability design has to open dialogue, she has a natural tendency to endeavour in multidisciplinary projects within the social, cultural and artistic sector.

Her main focus lies on editorial design and visual identity development although having a soft spot for anything motion-picture related. Her portfolio lives off of colourful, bold and playful approaches, and places great value on substantiation and research during each step of the way.
