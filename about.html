About
cHi mE No is a Lisbon based Communication Designer who seeks to bridge the gap between experimentation, structure and contemporaneity with her design practice. Enticed by the ability design has to open dialogue, she has a natural tendency to endeavour in multidisciplinary projects within the social, cultural and artistic sector. Although having a soft spot for research lead projects, her main focus is on editorial design and visual identity development which live off of colourful, bold and playful approaches.
