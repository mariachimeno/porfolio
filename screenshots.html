SCREENshots
2020 – Ongoing
*ੈ✩‧₊˚ Archive

SCREENshots is a digital archive of ideas that never came to fruition, mistakes, snippets of my creative process and a record of my design methodology. Above all, it is a — somewhat selfish and vulnerable — place of exploration where shapes, layout, typography and color meet in a chaotic and spontaneous conversation, which I decided to call Visual Stimuli.

This is an attempt of embracing the playfulness and boldness inherent to the practice of graphic design.
