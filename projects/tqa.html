The Quixotic Assembly
2023
Visual Identity, Editorial Design
.·:*¨¨* Academic Project

The Quixotic Assembly is a pluriversal gathering space that lives off the idea that one day, due to the rampant rise in technology and automation, labor will stop being the center of human activity.

TQA seeks to incite dialogue by not only reflecting on pain, but also discomfort, the unavoidable sense of helplessness, lack of meaning, desire and drive that comes with uncertainty and transformation.

Exhibited on How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb at Galerias Municipais, Pavilhão Branco, June 2023
