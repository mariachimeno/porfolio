Cinemateca
2021
Visual Identity, Communication Design
.·:*¨¨* Academic Project

The mission of Cinemateca Portuguesa - Museu do Cinema is to collect, protect, preserve and publicise the heritage related to moving images, promoting knowledge of the history of cinema and the development of film and audiovisual culture.

This rebrand answers to the need to update Cinemateca as an institution, appealing to a younger audience. In order to make that change, the whole identity was rethought, and new film cycles were implemented in order to reach and mantain the new public coming into this space. The new visual identity lives off of a pastel color scheme and both classic and contemporary elements, keeping in mind Cinemateca's history and existing audience.
