Skin Deep
2021
Editorial Design, Graphic Design
.·:*¨¨* Academic Project

Skin Deep is a newspaper that reflexts upon the idea of our identity as a fontier — between the inside and the outside, contruction and deconstruction. It aims to reach a wide, mostly young, audience with the intention of celebrating the queer identity loudly and proudly, whilst assuring a safe and inviting space for its readers. At its core, it defends the normalisation and dismistification of the queer community and self-identity, embracing it's fluidity and complexity.
