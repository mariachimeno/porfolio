5 Steps of Subversion
2021 – 2022
Editorial Design, Graphic Design
.·:*¨¨* Academic Project

5 Steps of Subversion is a multi-format publication that subverts the ever-rising encouragement of individualism. Each format explores concepts and dichotomies such as individual vs collective, singular vs plural, island vs archipelago, in hopes of sparking a conversation.

Exhibited on From Order to Chaos and Back in Galerias Municipais, Galeria Quadrum, June 2022
