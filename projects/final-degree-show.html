"(…) and we think, and plan, and dream, (…) and we hope for a better future" Corneliussen, C. (2021). Hello Humans, I'm Not a Chatbot

And We Think, and Plan, and Dream, and We Hope for a Better Future is a visual identity and naming proposal for ETIC's Final Degree Show for the Graphic Design graduates. This chosen name intends to reflect the students' use of artificial intelligence as a research base and/or tool for the projects they present in this exhibition. As this degree show is mainly speculative in nature, the proposed visual identity sets a futuristic mood that resorts to abstract-like elements and an unsual contrasting color palette.
