Zero
2021 – 2022
Visual Identity, Communication Design
.·:*¨¨* Academic Project

Rebrand for zero, a portuguese non-profit association that carries out its activities in complete independence from political parties, companies and profit-making organisations. zero believes in balancing the environment, society and economy while fully respecting the planet's natural limits.

This rebrand consisted in refreshing the previous identity, updating not only the logotype but also establishing a strong cromatic universe according to zero's fields of action. The new identity is supported by many stationary materials, as well as a print and digital communication strategy.

Developed in collaboration with Carolina Martins, Margarida Monteiro e Salomé Vasconcelos.
