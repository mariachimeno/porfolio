How I Learned to Stop
Worrying and Love the Bomb
Editorial Design & Communication Design
“The future is not what it used to be.” Kubrick, S. (1964). Dr. Strangelove, or, How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb.(Film)

Designers play a fundamental role in shaping the path towards a more inclusive, egalitarian, and sustainable society, challenging the boundaries of conventional knowledge and envisioning alternative narratives. Fostering a supportive culture, encouraging experimentation, and promoting knowledge-seeking beyond theoretical basis, this final degree exhibition brings together projects that have dared to imagine alternative models, challenge standards, and open new avenues of exploration.
